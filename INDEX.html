About Me

I am a Senior Technology Executive and Serial Co-founder with a career defined by building high-scale enterprise backends and successfully exiting them.

Most recently, I served as Senior Director of Engineering for AI Agents at Zendesk, following their acquisition of Ultimate.ai, where I was CTO. There, I led the technical strategy that transformed a startup into a market leader, scaling the team from 8 to 60 and eventually managing a department of 230+ engineers and researchers.

My background spans regulated HealthTech (Clue), TravelTech (Amadeus), and Enterprise SaaS. Whether it's securing FDA approval for a digital contraceptive or building real-time speech-to-speech AI models, I focus on engineering that delivers measurable business value.

I am now available for fractional CTO roles, helping the next wave of founders navigate the complex journey from Seed to Exit.
